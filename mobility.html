Mobility

With the aim of fulfilling target 11.2, we must be able to extend and expand the public transport network not just within Cyberjaya but inbound and outbound transportation into Cyberjaya as well.

Ways we aim to improve public transport

Electric bus operating around the city

By implementing an electric bus system around the city, not only it saves the environment by not releasing any pollutants, but it also encourages the reduced use of personal vehicles(like cars) as we hope that part of the current roads will be reserved specifically for these buses.

Rentable electric scooters around the city

With already a few small startups such as the BEAM electric scooters in Cyberjaya, our aim is to expand these network of scooters even further. By placing an electric scooter station every 1km allows the easy accessibility of scooters no matter where you are in the city. To all the citizens, we do hope this will be making good use of these facilities.
